    Arrays

    Introduction¶

    The basic data structure in computer science to hold collection of related data is an Array.

    Disadvantages of Arrays¶

    1. In an unordered array, searching is slow.
    2. In an ordered array, insertion is slow.
    3. In both ordered and unordered arrays, deletion is slow.
    4. They are never the right size. Once declared the size of an array can't be changed. They are either too big or too small for your dataset.
